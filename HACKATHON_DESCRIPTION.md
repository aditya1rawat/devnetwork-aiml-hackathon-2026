## 📋 Requirements & Rules

*View the Full Set of Instructions here!*

* **Format:** This is an in-person & online hackathon.
* **Team Formation:** Teams will be formed on Devpost.com. Participants can message each other to form teams, participate in chat boards, and communicate with sponsors.
* **Project Scope:** Teams can build apps from scratch (Mobile App / Web App).
* **Challenges:** Teams can choose to solve no challenges (build whatever you want) or submit to as many challenges as they want.
* **Judging Process:**
* **Round 1:** Judges will review all team projects and fill out a scoring sheet to determine the Overall Hackathon Winner.
* **Round 2 (Sponsor Round):** Each sponsor will judge the teams working on their Challenge(s) and determine their own prize winner(s).



## ⚖️ Judging Criteria

Round 1 judging will be based on the following:

* **Progress:** How much progress did you make on your submission?
* **Concept:** Does your submission solve a real problem?
* **Feasibility:** Could your submission grow/evolve to become a startup or be a profitable business?
