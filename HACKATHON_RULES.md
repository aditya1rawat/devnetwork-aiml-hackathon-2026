## Hackathon Rules

* **Project Scope:** Teams should build apps from scratch (Mobile App / Consumer Web App / Business Web App). Teams can solve as many “Challenges” as desired (zero, one, or multiple). Challenges are listed here: [https://aidevsummit.co/hackathon/](https://aidevsummit.co/hackathon/)
* **Team Size:** Teams must be between 1 - 5 members. Teams with over 5 members are not eligible to win anything.
* **Team Formation:** Participants are encouraged to form teams prior to the start of the Hackathon. Participants, from their DevPost accounts, may “Look for Teammates”.
* **Schedule:** Hackathon Workshops & Kickoff Talks will take place Monday, May 11 from 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM PST.
* **Sponsor Challenges:** All “Challenges” are hosted by sponsors. The sponsors will choose their own prize and choose their own prize winners at the conclusion of the hackathon. Read this sheet to understand how sponsors judge.
* **Deadline:** To be considered for the Overall Winner - teams must submit their projects by **Thursday, May 28 at 10:00 AM PST**. No late projects will be accepted.
* **Seating:** Once your project team sits at a table, DO NOT change tables. The table number taped down will be assigned to you for the Overall Judging - Round 1.
* **Judging Schedule:** There will be two rounds of judging on Wednesday. During the Overall Judging - Round 1 (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM PT), Overall Round Judges will review your projects and fill out a score card. The Sponsor Round - Round 2 (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM PT) Judges will review your projects and fill out a score card.
* **Overall Judges (Round 1) Criteria:**
* **Progress:** How much progress did you make?
* **Concept:** Does it solve a real problem?
* **Feasibility:** Could this become a startup or company?


* **Top 5:** The Top 5 project teams (as decided by the Overall Judges - Round 1) will pitch on the Main Stage to all attendees. You will be notified via phone call at 1:00 PM if you are selected in the Top 5 and must be onsite at 3:00 PM, for the 3:30 PM Top 5 Demo & Q&A.
* **Prizes & Announcements:** Sponsors are responsible for delivering their prizes to winners (usually immediately after). If needed, we will provide sponsors team contact information. Sponsor & Overall winners are announced at 3:30 PM on Thursday.

## Submission & Participation Steps

### A) REQUIRED: Register yourself on devpost.com

1. Go to [https://devnetwork-ai-ml-hack-2026.devpost.com/]()
2. Click the "Register for this hackathon" button.
3. Create a DevPost account (you will need to verify the email from your inbox). If you already have an account, just log in.
4. If creating an account, you will be prompted to set up a profile.
5. Before registration is complete, you will be asked a few quick questions about why you joined the hackathon. Once complete, click “Register”.
6. Once you have registered, you can send “Team-up Requests” to other participants if relevant.

### B) Determine what Sponsor Challenges/Prizes you want to work on

1. Review Sponsor Challenges/Prizes. You will find these at the bottom of the homepage of the DevNetwork [AI + ML] Hackathon 2026 DevPost page.

### C) REQUIRED: Create a Project

1. Make sure you and all team members have created an account on Devpost and have registered for the “DevNetwork [AI + ML] Hackathon 2026” here: [https://devnetwork-ai-ml-hack-2026.devpost.com/]()
2. Once on the hackathon homepage, click “Enter a Submission”.
3. You are then directed to a page where you can add a new project.
4. When adding a “New Project”, you will need to enter the below required information (Details can be edited at a later time):
**Required at time of creation:**
* a. What’s Your Project Called
* b. Here’s the Elevator Pitch
* c. Created By (Add your team members so they can edit the project with you)
* d. Here’s the Whole Story
**Recommended before submission deadline (This is NOT Required):**
* e. It’s Built With (Add what technologies you used)
* f. Image Gallery (screenshots of the project)
* g. Try It Out (A Link or URL to your project)
* h. Video Demo


5. Save Details.
6. On the next page, select what “Sponsor Challenges / Prizes” you have or plan to work on (can be zero or multiple).
7. Then, you will accept the terms and conditions and select “Submit to Hackathon”.
8. Please note, you can “Edit Project & Submission Details” until Thursday, May 29, at 10:00 AM PST.

### D) REQUIRED: Finalize & Submit your “Project”

*Finalize & Submit by 10:00 AM via the Devpost DevNetwork [AI + ML] Hackathon 2026 Website so you can be JUDGED.*

1. Finalize & Submit your “Project” by **Thursday, May 29, at 10:00 AM PST** to the “DevNetwork [AI + ML] Hackathon 2026” on DevPost.
2. Once you Submit Your Project, The Overall Round Judges and Sponsor Judges will review your project and judge you.
3. Projects submitted after 3:30 PM PST on **Thursday, May 28, 2026**, will not be accepted.

### E) Wait to see if you win

1. Sponsor Challenge Winners & Overall Winners will be announced at 3:30 PM on Thursday, May 28, 2026.
